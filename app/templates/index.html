Info

ResuMatch is AI-driven platform that instantly customizes your resume to any job description, combining efficieny with noticeabilty.

With everyday life moving fast and the job market even faster, most people don't have time to individually tailor their resume for every opportunity. Simply upload your resume and a given job description, and ResuMatch highlights the right skills, keywords, and experience to get past ATS and land that interview! Get Started Here.

Work

ResuMatch streamlines the resume customization process so users can focus on applying, not formatting . Our AI analyzes job descriptions and existing resumes to automatically suggest the most relevant skills, keywords, and phrasing for each position. The result: a tailored, ATS-friendly resume ready to impress employers in minutes.

About Us

ResuMatch was created by a group of engineering and computer/software students, all navigating the challenges of the modern job market. Experiencing firsthand how time-consuming and repetitive it can be to prepare resumes for multiple opportunities, we set out to build a solution that combines technical innovation with practical experience.

Our mission is to empower students and job seekers to navigate the application process more efficiently. By leveraging our knowledge in software development and AI, we aim to provide tools that make applying smarter, while helping users present their skills and experience with confidence.
